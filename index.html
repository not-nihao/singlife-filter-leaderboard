Information

Objective

Score the most amount of points within 1 minute by completing quests

Rules

Points are accumalated by completing quests as heroes.

Number of points per quest varies based on the difficulty of the quest.

The difficulty of a quest determines the amount of time that a user has to wait for the quest to complete (i.e. the higher the difficulty level, the greater the number of points per difficulty, but number of quests able to complete in 1 minute becoes lesser).

Wait time can be reduced further depending on the experience level of the hero and the trait of the hero

During the game, users can switch between which hero he/she wants to play as.

Each hero has its own unique stamina level, class (aka special ability).

Every hero also has a base experiece level of 0, that can be increased by consuming experience potions.

When a hero completes a quest, its stamina level drops by 10.

When a hero's stamina level reaches 0, it becomes unuasble.

However its stamina level can be regenerated back to full after consuming a stamina potion.

During the duration of the game, users can go to the shop to buy items such as stamina potions using the points they have accumalated as currency.
Note that there is a cap on how many of each item can be bought from the shop.

Items bought from the shop, can be found in the inventory.

The user can also use the items by entering their inventory.

Technicalities

Points: Quest reward; In-game currency

Hero: Character used to complete quests

Class: The type of ability that the hero possesses(Strength, Intelligence, Healing, Speed)

Stamina: Determines how much fight left in the Hero (reduces by 20 each quest)

Experience: Reduces wait time by 10% for each level increased

Trait: Wait time reduced by 10% if trait matches hero class

Difficulty: Ranges from 1 to 5 (1 = least difficult; 5 = most difficulty)

Wait Time: How long a user has to wait before he can move on to the next quest

Price: Cost of item in shop

Remaining: Stock of Item left (Max limit of 5)

Count: Number of items left of each item in the inventory (Max limit of 5)
